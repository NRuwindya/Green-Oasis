Who are we?

Green Oasis was founded in 2024 with a mission to bring the beauty and benefits of plants to homes and workplaces. We are a dedicated team of plant enthusiasts who believe in the transformative power of greenery. Our goal is to make plant ownership easy, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our values

  • Sustainability: Sourcing plants from eco-friendly suppliers and promoting environmentally conscious practices.
  • Quality: Ensuring each plant is healthy and ready to thrive in its new home.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Providing excellent customer service and support to help our customers succeed in their plant journeys.
  • Education: Educating our customers on plant care and the environmental benefits of greenery.

Why choose us?

Choosing Green Oasis means choosing a partner in your plant journey. We offer a wide variety of plants suitable for different environments and care levels, from low-maintenance succulents to exotic tropical plants. Our user-friendly online shop makes it easy to browse, select, and order plants, with fast and secure delivery. Additionally, our customer service team is always here to provide advice and support, ensuring that your plants flourish.
